HOTFIX v1.0.4.1 - TIEBREAKER TEXT MISSING
==========================================
Date: February 11, 2026
Fix Type: UI Bug
Severity: Low (cosmetic)

ISSUE:
======
Manual entry page (for host to enter paper submissions) was missing
the tiebreaker explanation text below the input box.

Players could see:
- "Tiebreaker Guess (0-100):"
- [Input box]
- [No explanation of what the tiebreaker means]

EXPECTED:
=========
Should show explanation text below input:
"How many people out of the 100 surveyed by Family Feud gave 
the #1 answer? (You do not have to have answer on your sheet)"

ROOT CAUSE:
===========
The play.html template (for players) had the explanation text.
The manual_entry.html template (for host) was missing it.

THE FIX:
========
Added <p> tag with explanation text to manual_entry.html:

<p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.8); margin-top: 8px; font-size: 0.9em;">
    How many people out of the 100 surveyed by Family Feud gave 
    the #1 answer? (You do not have to have answer on your sheet)
</p>

FILES MODIFIED:
===============
- templates/manual_entry.html (added explanation text)
- templates/host.html (version bump to v1.0.4.1)

TESTING:
========
✅ App imports successfully
✅ Template renders correctly
✅ Text appears below tiebreaker input

IMPACT:
=======
Low - This was purely a missing help text issue.
Does not affect gameplay or scoring.
Only affects host manual entry page clarity.

VERSION:
========
v1.0.4 → v1.0.4.1 (hotfix)
